Strategy

Challenge & Approach

The Challenge

The website needed to serve a dual purpose: inspire potential franchise partners with the business opportunity, while showcasing the emotional customer experience that makes the concept unique.

  • Translate an in-store tactile experience into a digital format
  • Speak to two distinct audiences: partners & end customers
  • Operate across 7 languages for international tourist locations
  • Build trust quickly with a conversion-oriented structure

The Approach

We centered the design around emotion and proof. Real customer photos, partner testimonials, and a video walkthrough replace typical corporate copy with authentic human moments.

  • Emotion-first design: let real moments tell the story
  • Progressive disclosure from concept to business details
  • Mobile-first responsive architecture for on-the-go visitors
  • Lightweight vanilla stack for maximum performance

Key Features

Designed with Purpose

Every feature was carefully considered to support both emotional engagement and business conversion.

Memory Creation UX

The concept revolves around a creative experience where visitors compose personalized illustrated avatars of their family, friends, or pets.

7-Language i18n System

A custom-built modular internationalization engine supporting French, English, Italian, Spanish, Portuguese, German, and Dutch.

Performance First

Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS with zero framework overhead. Cloudinary CDN for media, WebP images, and optimized loading for mobile connections.

Mobile-First Design

Responsive architecture with custom mobile navigation, touch-friendly interactions, and fluid typography using CSS clamp().

Modern UI Effects

Glassmorphism navigation, animated gradient buttons, parallax hero, scroll-reveal animations, and micro-interactions throughout.

Conversion-Oriented

Strategic CTA placement, social proof sections, partner testimonials, and a clear path from discovery to franchise application.

Real Moments

250,000+ Portraits. Real Smiles.

Copywriting Strategy

Words That Resonate, Not Just Inform

The copy was crafted to mirror the emotional experience of the store. Instead of corporate language, we used warm, conversational tone that places the reader in the moment.

Headlines like "More than a portrait. An experience." immediately set the emotional context. Partner testimonials use direct quotes for authenticity, and CTAs are framed as invitations rather than commands.

Every text element was translated into 7 languages with cultural adaptation, not just direct translation.

Information Architecture

From Curiosity to Commitment

The site follows a deliberate narrative arc. The homepage builds from emotional hook (hero + video) to social proof (gallery + testimonials) to rational justification (concept details + partner benefits) to action (CTA + application).

Secondary pages allow deeper exploration: the Concept page breaks down the in-store process, the Network page provides franchise details, and the Partner page streamlines the application flow.
